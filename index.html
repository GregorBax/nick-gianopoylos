ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm Nikos Giannopoulos from Thessaloniki, Greece. I have worked as a Data Science Intern at NET2GRID Hellas where I was responsible for the Creation of strategy to gather, utilize and store energy data to an appropriate dataset. Furthermore, I have worked on Machine Learning algorithms for real-time disaggregation and recognition of appliance models. My experience comes maintly from volunteerism, as I have been involved in TEDx University of Macedonia project as a web developer as well as in IEEE Aristotle University of Thessaloniki Student Branch as Machine Learning Study Group Coordinator. I gained programming experience in many different languages and framework such as C, C++, Java, R, SPSS and MATLAB. However, what I enjoy the most is working with Python in the field of Machine Learning and Big Data.

I have graduated from Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, Greece with a Five-year Diploma of Unified Circle Studies (Master's Degree) with Final Degree Grade: 7.99 out of 10 in Electrical and Computer Engineering and I have conquered the 3rd place at the European Best Engineering Competition 2015.
